Welcome to GMAT Cards Project.

Things are simple here. The project is hosted on Heroku under this project's name. The index page contains a list of card groups. Clicking on any one of them will open up a random card from the group. Click the card once to get the meaning, click it second time, and move onto the next card.

At the bottom of each card you will find some basics stats and a Home button, which will allow you to select a different group.
